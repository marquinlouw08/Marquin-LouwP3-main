Welcome to the about page, we are a family owned local bakery in the streets of Cape Town.

Our mission is to provide freshly baked bread, pastries and cakes on a daily basis to fulfill the needs of our community.

Our Story

Jason Bakery was founded in 2003 by Jason and his wife, Sarah. They started with a small bakery in the heart of Cape Town using traditional recipes passed down through generations. Over the years their dedication to quality and customer satisfaction has helped us grow into one of the most beloved bakeries in the city. We are known for traditionally making breads and baked goods made using only the best local ingredients. All of our products are handmade by our bakers everyday using local flour, salt and filtered water.

Jason's knowledge and experience is what sets Jason breads apart from the rest. Honesty and integrity has become important to our business and we are committed to maintaining these values in everything we do by consistently delivering quality over quantity. Everything we have on display we bake today to be consumed today. We do not rush making our products as we believe in practicing patience in order to fill the needs of our customers which is why we stay persistent on delivering fresh products on a daily basis because we are committed to serving our community’s needs.
